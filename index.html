How It Works

Capture, AI Grades, Teacher Reviews — Three Steps

Teachers always have final say. AI just makes the process 10x faster.

01

Upload Papers

Take photos or upload PDFs. The system uses OCR and layout parsing to automatically recognize questions and student responses.

02

AI Grading

Objective items scored automatically. Subjective items get rubric-based draft scores and comments, color-coded by confidence level.

03

Teacher Review

Review and approve AI suggestions in a streamlined queue. All final scores and feedback are teacher-approved with a full audit trail.

Traffic Light System

Smart Triage, Focused Attention

AI assigns a confidence level to every subjective grading result. Teachers focus only on items that need human judgment, dramatically reducing review time.

High Confidence — One-click approve
Medium Confidence — Quick review, then approve or adjust
Low Confidence — Requires careful teacher review
